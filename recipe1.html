Fluffy Scrambled Eggs and Avocado Slices on Toast

Total Time: 10 Minutes

Recipe Info:

  • Course: Breakfast
  • Cuisine: American
  • Servings: 1

Ingredient Amount Converter

Ingredients:

  • • Cooking spray
  • • 2 eggs
  • • 0.5 tbsp milk
  • • Sea salt and freshly cracked pepper to taste
  • • 1 pieces of bread toasted & buttered
  • • Avocado sliced

Instructions:

  • 1. Heat the cooking spray in a skillet over medium heat. Whisk the eggs together with the milk, sea salt, and freshly cracked pepper, to taste.
  • 2. Pour in the egg mixture. Constantly fold the egg mixture from the bottom of the pan to the top. This will keep it from overcooking and the middle section will stay soft and fluffy. Do this constantly for 1 or 2 minutes, or until the eggs are cooked to your liking.
  • 3. Meanwhile, place your bread into the toaster and toast; butter the toast when finished. Slice the avocado into slices.
  • 4. Layer the scrambled egg on top of the buttered toast followed by slices of avocado. Season with sea salt and freshly cracked pepper, to taste. Serve immediately. Enjoy!
