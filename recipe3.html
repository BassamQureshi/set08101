Teriyaki Chicken and Vegetable Rice Bowl

Cook Time: 40 minutes

Recipe Info:

  • Course: Dinner
  • Cuisine: Asian
  • Serves: 1
  • Prep Time: 15 Minutes

Ingredients:

    • 0.5 tsp toasted sesame seeds

    • 1.25 boneless skinless chicken thighs, trimmed of any excess fat

    • 0.06 cup soy sauce

    • 0.5 tbsp brown sugar

    • 0.5 tbsp chicken broth

    • 0.5 tbsp rice vinegar

    • 0.5 cloves of garlic, minced

    • 0.25 tsp fresh ginger, minced

    • 0.13 tsp sesame oil

    • 0.06 tsp crushed red pepper flakes

    Rice:

    • 0.5 tsp butter

    • 1 cloves of garlic, minced

    • 0.19 cup Jasmine rice, rinsed

    • 0.38 cups chicken broth **You can also use veg broth or water

    • Sea salt and white pepper, to taste

    • 0.25 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped, if desired

    Vegetables:

    • 0.5 tsp vegetable oil

    • 0.5 tsp toasted sesame oil

    • Pinch of crushed red pepper flakes, to taste

    • 0.06 red onion, thickly sliced

    • 0.25 cup broccoli florets

    • 0.13 bell pepper, sliced

    • 0.25 cup shiitake mushrooms, sliced

    • 0.25 cup snow peas, trimmed

    • 0.25 cup of carrot matchsticks

    • 0.5 cloves of garlic, minced

    • Sea salt and freshly cracked pepper, to taste

    Serving:

    • Toasted sesame seeds

    • Soy Sauce

    • Sriracha sauce

Instructions:

    Marinate the Chicken:

    1. Combine the soy sauce, sugar, chicken broth, vinegar, garlic, ginger, sesame oil, and red pepper flakes in a large zip lock bag. Mix until well combined. Add the trimmed chicken thighs to the marinade, seal the bag, and place them in the refrigerator for up to 4 hours.

    Prepare the Rice:

    1. Heat the butter and olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook, stirring constantly, for 1 minute. Add the rice and cook, stirring often so the rice doesn’t stick to the pan, for 3-4 minutes. Add the chicken broth and season with sea salt and white pepper, to taste. Bring to a boil then reduce heat to medium-low.

    2. Cover and let cook for 20 minutes. Remove the rice from the burner without removing the lid and let it sit for 5 minutes. Carefully remove the lid, add the parsley, and fluff rice with a fork.

    Prepare the Chicken & Vegetables:

    1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat then coat with cooking spray. Once the pan is hot, add the chicken thighs and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side or until the chicken is cooked through. Let the meat rest for a few minutes then slice.

    2. While the chicken is cooking, heat the canola oil, sesame oil, and crushed red pepper flakes in a large sauté pan over medium-high heat. Add the red onion, broccoli, red bell pepper, and shiitake mushrooms; cook, stirring often, for 2-3 minutes

    3. Add the snow peas and carrot; cook, stirring often, for 1 minute. Add the minced garlic then season with sea salt and freshly cracked pepper, to taste and cook, stirring constantly, for 30-45 seconds.

    4. Spoon rice into each bowl then top with chicken slices and veggies sprinkle the top with toasted sesame seeds. Serve with soy sauce on the side, if desired. Enjoy!
